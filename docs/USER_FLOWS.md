# User Flows

## Spectator

Goal: reach services or a seat safely.

1. Open the crowd heatmap or navigation view.
2. Select an origin, destination, and accessibility preference.
3. Follow the generated route or ask the multilingual concierge.

Outcome: clear, inclusive guidance that avoids known pressure points.

## Volunteer

Goal: direct guests confidently during busy periods.

1. Review the current crowd heatmap.
2. Use navigation to verify the safest available route.
3. Share the guidance with the spectator.

Outcome: consistent frontline support informed by live simulation data.

## Emergency Operator

Goal: assess and mitigate a developing crowd issue.

1. Review predictions and high-risk zones on the dashboard.
2. Evaluate the AI-generated recommendation and its affected zones.
3. Approve or reject the action, recording a decision note.

Outcome: faster decisions with an auditable human approval gate.

## Security Personnel

Goal: maintain operational accountability.

1. Sign in with authorised staff credentials.
2. Review recommendation history and audit records.
3. Use the record to coordinate follow-up actions.

Outcome: traceable decisions with role-based access.

## Transport Coordinator

Goal: reduce arrival congestion and travel emissions.

1. Open the transport advisor.
2. Compare time, cost, and emissions across travel options.
3. Promote the suitable lower-impact option to attendees.

Outcome: more sustainable, better-distributed arrivals.

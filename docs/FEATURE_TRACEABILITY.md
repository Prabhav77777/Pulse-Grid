# Feature Traceability Matrix

| Capability | Challenge need | Primary user | Outcome | Value |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Crowd heatmap and prediction | Detect crowd pressure before it becomes unsafe | Safety officer | Prioritised zone interventions | Reduces congestion risk |
| Accessible routing | Move spectators safely through a complex venue | Spectator, volunteer | Step-free or shortest route | Inclusive wayfinding |
| AI operations recommendations | Turn deterministic signals into actionable decisions | Operations commander | Human-reviewed mitigation actions | Faster, accountable response |
| Approval and audit trail | Keep operational decisions transparent | Security lead | Approved or rejected recommendation record | Human control and traceability |
| Multilingual concierge | Deliver venue guidance to international visitors | Spectator | Context-aware answers in four languages | Better fan experience |
| Sustainable transport advisor | Reduce event travel emissions and congestion | Transport coordinator | Low-impact travel options | Supports greener operations |
| Operations report | Learn from each matchday | Tournament management | Exportable operational summary | Continuous improvement |

The simulation layer calculates routes and crowd predictions deterministically. GenAI explains those results; it never makes autonomous operational changes.
